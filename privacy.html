Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Luna Through History content-publishing tool ("the Service") handles data.

1. Who this applies to

The Service is an internal tool used solely by Luna Through History to publish its own content to its own social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). It is not a public-facing product and does not collect data from visitors of this website.

2. Data accessed via platform APIs

The Service uses official platform APIs (such as TikTok's Content Posting API) only to authenticate and publish video content to accounts owned by Luna Through History. Any account information or access tokens obtained through these APIs are used exclusively for this publishing purpose and are stored securely, accessible only to the account owner.

3. No sharing with third parties

Data obtained through platform APIs is never sold, shared, or used for advertising. It is not used for any purpose beyond publishing Luna Through History's own content.

4. Data retention

Access tokens are retained only for as long as needed to operate the publishing tool and can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the account through the relevant platform's settings.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to luna.through.history@gmail.com.
